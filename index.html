About The Event

About The Event

The Spotlight festival is a highly anticipated event that brings together music enthusiasts from all over the world for a multi-day celebration of live performances, light shows, and immersive experiences across multiple stages and genres, creating a vibrant and inclusive atmosphere that fosters a sense of community and shared passion for music.

Where

Klokgebouw, PSV Stadion & Philips Museum - Eindhoven

When

Friday to Sunday
7-9 Juli
