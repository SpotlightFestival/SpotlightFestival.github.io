Frank Rizardo

Franky Rizardo is a highly talented and accomplished DJ and producer who has made a significant impact on the house music scene. Hailing from the Netherlands, Rizardo has gained a reputation for his infectious energy and seamless blending of various house subgenres, from deep and tech house to disco and soulful vibes.

With a career spanning over a decade, Rizardo has released a string of successful tracks on renowned labels such as Defected, Relief, and Rejected. His productions are characterized by groovy basslines, catchy hooks, and intricate percussion, creating a captivating and dancefloor-friendly sound that has earned him a loyal following.

Rizardo's DJ sets are renowned for their impeccable flow and ability to keep crowds moving. His infectious enthusiasm and dynamic mixing skills have taken him to prestigious venues and festivals around the world, solidifying his reputation as a skilled entertainer and crowd-pleaser. With his undeniable talent and dedication to the craft, Franky Rizardo continues to make waves in the house music scene and leave a lasting impression on both listeners and fellow artists.
